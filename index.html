Abstract

[Abstract Placeholder] Echocardiography video segmentation is critical for diagnosing cardiovascular diseases. However, existing methods often struggle with speckle noise, low contrast, and substantial shape deformations throughout the cardiac cycle.

In this paper, we propose GDKVM, a novel Spatiotemporal Key-Value Memory network equipped with a Gated Delta Rule. Unlike traditional memory networks that rely on static addressing, GDKVM introduces a dynamic update mechanism that selectively retains relevant historical information while filtering out noise. Extensive experiments on public benchmarks demonstrate that our method achieves state-of-the-art performance in segmentation accuracy and temporal consistency.
